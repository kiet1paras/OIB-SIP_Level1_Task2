Bhuvneshwar Kumar

1990 - Present

Swing King of India

Bhuvneshwar Kumar Singh (born 5 February 1990) is an Indian international cricketer known for his ability to swing the ball both ways. A right-arm medium-fast bowler, he made his debut for India in 2012 and quickly gained recognition for his accuracy, control, and consistency. He is also known for his contributions in limited-overs formats and has been a valuable asset in the Indian Premier League (IPL), especially with Sunrisers Hyderabad.

Bhuvneshwar is renowned for being the first Indian bowler to take a five-wicket haul in all three formats of the game.     2. Swing bowling is a technique used by fast bowlers in cricket to move the ball laterally through the air before it reaches the batsman. It is especially effective under overcast conditions or with a slightly old ball.
